About King Trading

King Trading Co. is a global leader in energy and commodity trading, specializing in oil, marine fuels, LPG, metals, and more. With operations spanning across major international hubs, we provide seamless logistics, high-quality products, and trusted partnerships worldwide.

Our Trading Portfolio

  • Crude Oil: Seamless global crude logistics with flexible supply solutions serving refiners worldwide.
  • Marine Fuels: Premium marine bunker fuels from our strategically located port terminals.
  • LPG & Gas: Supplying pressurized LPG and natural gas to emerging markets across Asia and Africa.
  • Metals & Minerals: Global trading in aluminum, copper, nickel, and industrial ferroalloys.
  • Distillates & Naphtha: Reliable distribution networks supporting fuel and petrochemical industries.
  • Dry Bulk Cargo: Comprehensive dry bulk trade including coal, bauxite, and pet coke.
